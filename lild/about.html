About page

This web application is built for the main purpose of assisting patients and pharmarcies find each other easily. We understand that when most people are ill and need drugs urgently, they walk into different pharmaceutical stores till they find their particular meds, MedLocator is the problem-solving tool in this case because it helps ill people search for their meds from the comfort of their homes.



It uses navigation to locate nearby pharmacies for patients looking for meds and also helps registered pharmacies sell their medicines easier to the people in need of them.
